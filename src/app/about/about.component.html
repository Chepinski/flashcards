Epiquiz was created with the intention of building a user friendly database for studying code, specifically the fundamentals of code from the course work at Epicodus.


"Flashcards can bear vocabulary, historical dates, formulas or any subject matter that can be learned via a question-and-answer format. Flashcards are widely used as a learning drill to aid memorization by way of spaced repetition."

As aspiring developers, we created Epiquiz to be a tool for ourselves that could be expanded to include other subjects and be available for a wider audience. We wish you luck in your studies!
